
  Analysis of bulkRNA seq data from primary human macrophages edited via nucleofection vs. Cas9EDV for AAVS1 loci.

  Bulk RNA seq analysis 

  Fastq files submitted to GEO
  
  Groups: NTD, Nucleofection (gAAVS1), and Cas9-EDV (gAAVS1), labeled as NTD, Nuc, and EDV, respectively.
  
  Bulk RNA-seq was performed on cells from four human donors, labeled as d1, d2.
  
  Samples were labeled based on the harvest time: D1 and D7 indicate cells collected on Day 1 and Day 7 from Day 0 edited samples, respectively, while D47 indicates cells   collected on Day 7 from Day 4 edited samples.
